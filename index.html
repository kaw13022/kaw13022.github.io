Short Bio

Innovation, Technology, Research, Application

Dr. Kai Wang received his Ph.D. in Transportation & Urban Engineering from the University of Connecticut in 2016. He holds two Master's degrees, with one in Transportation Engineering from the South Dakota State University, and the other one in Computational Data Analytics from the Georgia Institute of Technology. Dr. Wang joined the Connecticut Transportation Safety Research Center at the University of Connecticut in 2016, and is currently working as an assistant research professor and a project manager to conduct traffic safety and data science related research. Dr. Wang has extended knowledge in applying State-of-the-art statistical and economical methodologies and advanced analytical techniques to conduct transportation data analytics. Dr. Wang’s research interests are on the areas of statistical modeling, machine learning and deep learning, big data technologies, data mining and artificial intelligence. Dr. Wang is currently serving as a PI/Co-PI on multiple NCHRP projects as well as projects sponsored by USDOT, FHWA and State DOTs.
